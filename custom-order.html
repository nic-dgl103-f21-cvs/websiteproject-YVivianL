Custom Order

I usually draw characters inspired by video games, media, and other original characters. My art style is quite simplistic if you are expecting hyper realistic drawings or art then that’s not what you are going to find here. If I do get a request for a custom order, I’d prefer to have some visual reference and/or written descriptions. If you don’t get a reply from me within 3 business days, please contact me again.
