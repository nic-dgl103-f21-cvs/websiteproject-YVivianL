About me

I am a Taiwanese-Canadian freelance artist from Vancouver Island. I started making art and getting into art when I was around 10 years old. Honestly as a kid I use to draw a lot of Pokémon and comics that I thought were cool. I used to only use pencil and ink to draw all my sketches, then I started going digital with my art when I was in grade 11. I’m now a Business and design major making art because I am passionate about character design.
