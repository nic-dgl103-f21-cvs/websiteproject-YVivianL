Introduction

My name is Yu-hsuan Vivian Lai, and I'm a digital artist that enjoys creating passionate digital art. I spend most of my free time creating and designing characters inspired by-and originating from: video games, anime, and other media. I use the software Procreate to create all of my work.

My art style

My art style is very simple and clean. I generally use a very vibrant colour palette; however I also enjoy using stark contrasts with small splashes of colour to highlight some of my characters more prominent features such as the character on the left.

Art & Prices

I generally take about 3-5 hours creating the more simplistic pieces but for more complex works I could take anywhere from 5-15 hours. Prices change accordingly with the time estimated to complete each individual commision.
